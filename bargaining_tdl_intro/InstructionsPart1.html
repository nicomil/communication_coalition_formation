In this part of the experiment, you and the other participants will be randomly matched in groups of three. Each participant will be assigned a unique color label to ensure complete anonymity. You and the other two members of the group can choose how each of you would like to divide $ 12 between the three of you. Each player has the same three options to choose how divide the $ 12; one option equally splits the $ 12 among all the group members (this option assigns $ 4 to each player in the group), and the other two options equally split the $ 12 with only one of the other two players (this option assigns $ 6 to you and $ 6 to only one of the other two players). Before choosing the preferred option, you and the other participants in your group can freely communicate for a maximum of 10 minutes using the two chat-boxes on the screen.

Please note: The option on which at least two members of the group agree is paid out for all the group members. If no agreement is reached, all the group members earn $ 0.

For Example:

Imagine you are the GREEN player, and in your group, there are the RED and BLUE players. You can choose the option to divide the $12 equally among all the members of the group, and in this case, each of you can earn $ 4 if at least one other player in the group chooses the same option ( i.e., you are the GREEN player, and your choice is to equally split the $ 12 among all the members of the group; the RED player chooses to equally split the $ 12 among all the members of the group; the BLUE player chooses to equally split the $ 12 with only the RED player). Otherwise, if no players choose the same option, all the members of the group will earn $ 0 (i.e., you are the GREEN player, and your choice is to equally split the $ 12 among all the members of the group; the RED player chooses to equally split the $ 12 with only you; the BLUE player chooses to equally split the $ 12 with only the RED player). On the other hand, you can choose the option to divide the $ 12 equally with only one of the two members of the group. In this case, you and the other player can earn $ 6 if both of you choose to agree on the same option (i.e., You are the GREEN player, and your choice is to equally split the $ 12 only with the RED player; the RED player chooses to equally split the $ 12 only with you GREEN player; the BLUE player chooses to equally split the $ 12 among all the members of the group). Otherwise, if no players choose the same option, all the members of the group will earn $ 0 (i.e., you are the GREEN player, and your choice is to equally split the $ 12 among all the members of the group; the RED player chooses to equally split the $ 12 with only you; the BLUE player chooses to equally split the $ 12 with only the RED player).

Chat Instructions

Before choosing your option to divide the $ 12, you have maximum 10 minutes to communicate freely with the other participants. Messages are private, and you will only see the messages sent by you, and received by the each of the other group members. You will not see the messages sent between the other two group members. Before choosing your option you can write your messages, to each of the other two group members. In these messages you can explain to each of them what division you intend to choose, and what division you think they should choose. You can write different messages to the different group members. Before proceeding with your decision on how to divide the $ 12, you will be asked to briefly communicate your intentions to the other participants using a set of pre-written messages. As with the messages of the chat, your intentions will only be visible to the specific participant they are addressed to.

After the chat period, before choosing your option on how to divide the $ 12, is required to send your intentions choosing one pre-written message, to each of the other two group members. The pre-written messages to choose your intentions are the following:

  • Message 1: I wish to split the $ 12 equally with you only.
  • Message 2: I wish to split the $ 12 equally with the other player only.
  • Message 3: I wish to split the $ 12 equally with both the two players.

Please note: Revealing your identity to other participants, and using, discriminatory or offensive language, are strictly prohibited. Conversations will be analyzed after the end of the experimental session and before payments are finalized. If it is found that the above conditions have been violated, your payment for this experiment will be completely forfeited. We expect and appreciate your cooperation in maintaining a keeping this polite.

Summary

In summary, Part 1 will proceed as follows:

  1. Chat with each of the other members of the group.
  2. After the chat, each member of the group declares its intentions addressed to each of the other members of the group.
  3. After declaring their intentions, each member of the group makes their final decision on how they would like to split the $ 12.
  4. Once the final decision has been made, payments are determined. Payments will be communicated at the end of the experiment.
