In this part of the experiment, you and the other participants will be randomly matched in groups of three. You and the other two members of your group will choose how each of you would like to divide $12 between the three of you.

Each player has three options: to share the money equally with the player on the left, with the player on the right, or with both players.

For example:

  • Option 1: Share only with the player on the left.
  • Option 2: Share only with the player on the right.
  • Option 3: Share with both players.

Please note: If two, or all of you, agree on how the money will be divided, the money will be paid out accordingly. If, however, no two group members choose the same division, each of you will receive $0.

Chat Instructions

In this Part of the experiment, you will have to communicate freely with the other participants. You will have to write your messages to each of the other two group members. In these messages you can explain to each of them what division you intend to choose, and what division you think they should choose. You can write different messages to the different group members. Messages are private, and you will only see the messages sent by you, and received by the each of the other group members. You will not see the messages sent between the other two group members.

After the chat period, before proceeding with your decision on how to distribute the available $ 12, you will be asked to briefly communicate your intentions. You will have to communicate your intentions to each of the other participants using a set of pre-written messages, one for each member of the group. As with the messages of the chat, these pre-written messages will only be visible to the specific participant they are addressed to. The pre-written messages to choose your intentions are the following:

  • Message 1: I wish to split the $ 12 equally with you only.
  • Message 2: I wish to split the $ 12 equally with the other player only.
  • Message 3: I wish to split the $ 12 equally with both the two players.

Please note: Revealing your identity to other participants, and using, discriminatory or offensive language, are strictly prohibited. Conversations will be analyzed after the end of the experimental session and before payments are finalized. If it is found that the above conditions have been violated, your payment for this experiment will be completely forfeited. We expect and appreciate your cooperation in maintaining a keeping this polite.

Summary

In summary, Part 1 will proceed as follows:

  1. Write your messages with each of the other members of the group.
  2. After the communication, each member of the group chooses its intentions addressed to each of the other members of the group.
  3. After choosing their intentions, each member of the group makes their final decision, choosing how they would like to split the $ 12.
  4. Once the final decision has been made, payments are determined. Payments will be communicated at the end of the experiment.
