Welcome!

Thank you for taking part in this experiment!

In this experiment, you and the other participants will make a series of decisions that will directly affect your monetary rewards. Your earnings will depend on both your own choices and those made by the other participants. Similarly, your decisions will play a role in determining the earnings of others. All the data are anonymous, and we only ask for your honest responses.

The experiment consists of three parts: in Part 1: you can earn between $0 and $6 in a group choice task. In Part 2, you can earn an additional $5 ($0 otherwise) choosing between pairs of options. Finally, in Part 3, you can earn between $0 and $6 based on a group choice. Note, you will only be paid for either Part 1 or Part 3. At the end of the experiment, you will be informed, privately on your screen, of your earnings.

Please remember that your final earnings, at the end of this experiment, will be equal to a participation fee plus a bonus up to $5 plus your earnings from Part 1 or Part 3. Only one between Part 1 and Part 3, will be randomly selected with equal probability at the end of the experiment.
