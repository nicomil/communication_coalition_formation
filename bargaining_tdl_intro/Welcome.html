Welcome!

Thank you for taking part in this experiment!

In this experiment, you and the other participants will make a series of decisions that will directly affect your monetary rewards. Your earnings will depend on both your own choices and those made by the other participants. Similarly, your decisions will play a role in determining the earnings of others. All the data are anonymous, and we only ask for your honest responses. The experiment consists of two parts: in either Part 1 and Part 2, you can earn between $ 0 and $ 6 in a group choice task.

Note: you will only be paid for either Part 1 or Part 2.
