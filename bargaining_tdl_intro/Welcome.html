Welcome!

Thank you for taking part in this experiment!

In this experiment, you and the other participants will make a series of decisions that will directly affect your monetary rewards. Your earnings will depend on both your own choices and those made by the other participants. Similarly, your decisions will play a role in determining the earnings of others. All the data are anonymous, and we only ask for your honest responses.

The experiment consists of three parts: in Part 1, you can earn between $ 0 and $ 6 in a group choice task. In Part 2, you can earn an additional $ 5 ($ 0 otherwise) choosing between pairs of options. Finally, in Part 3, you can earn between $ 0 and $ 6 in a group choice task. Note, you will only be paid for either Part 1 or Part 3.

Please remember that your earnings will be equal to a participation fee of $ 2 plus an additional $ 5 from Part 2, plus your earnings from Part 1 or Part 3. Only one between Part 1 and Part 3, will be randomly selected with equal probability at the end of the experiment.
