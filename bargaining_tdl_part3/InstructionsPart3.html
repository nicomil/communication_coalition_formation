In this part of the experiment, you and the other participants will be asked how each of you would like to divide $ 12 between yourself ( SENDER ) and other two person ( RECEIVERS ). Each player has the same two options to choose how to divide the $ 12 ; one option equally splits the $ 12 between yourself and the other two players (this option assigns $ 4 either to the SENDER and the RECEIVERS), and the other option equally split the $ 12 with only one of the other two players (this option assigns $ 6 to the SENDER and $ 6 to only one of the RECEIVERS).

Please note that in Part 2 of the experiment, each participant will be randomly matched with other group members than those they were matched with in Part 1.

  • Option 1: Split equally the $ 12 with both the two players.
  • Option 2: Split equally the $ 12 with only one of the two players.

If Part 2 is randomly selected to determine the payoff of the experiment, then the choice made by one of the SENDER would be randomly selected and implemented for real payment.
