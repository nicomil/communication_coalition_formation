Please note: With this question, you and the other participants can earn an additional $ 2.

Each player selects an integer number between 0 and 100, the computer calculates the average of all selected numbers (i.e., the numbers are summed up and divided by the number of participants). Then, the average is multiplied by 0.6 to determine the winning value. The participant whose number is closest to the winning value wins and receives $ 2. If the numbers of several participants are equally close to the winning value, the winner will be randomly selected by the computer among those participants.

Example with five participants:

Participant One chooses 70, Participant Two chooses 20, Participant Three chooses 30, Participant Four chooses 45, and Participant Five chooses 60. The average of the five chosen numbers is 45. The average multiplied by 0.60 yields 27. The number of Participant Three (30) is closest to 27. Participant Three wins and receives $ 2.

Participant One chooses 80, Participant Two chooses 31, Participant Three chooses 35, Participant Four chooses 60, and Participant Five chooses 44. The average of the five chosen numbers is 50. The average multiplied by 0.60 yields 33. The numbers of Participant Two (31) and Participant Three (35) are equally close to 33. A random draw will determine the winner between the two participants, who will receive $ 2.
